ICT's Virtual Reality Theater

The ICT's Virtual Reality Theater provides a 3D graphics and sound environment unlike anything commercially available today. The surround sound system employs ten audio channels and two subwoofer channels. The sound effects are spatialized to give the impression of total immersion, and the demonstration they include things like the sounds of driving inside a Humvee (complete with creaks, bumps and rattles), the murmur of a crowd of people gathered in a Balkan village, and the sound of a Blackhawk helicopter flying overhead.
